Haute Couture & Shipping FAQs

Private fitting sessions can be booked by filling out the styling consultation form inside the "Consultation" tab, or by submitting a styling ticket on the right. Sizing consultations usually require 60 minutes.

Yes, 100% of our garments are designed and assembled in our San Francisco and Paris ateliers. We ensure fair-wage studio operations and source certified organic silk and upcycled wool directly from small heritage mills.

We provide full bespoke tailoring options for all couture blazers and silk dresses. Once your metrics are submitted, garments are shipped internationally via carbon-neutral express services in bio-degradable luxury packaging.

Since our pieces are handcrafted to order, we do not accept general returns. However, we offer complimentary adjustments on all private styling orders within 30 days of shipment to ensure a flawless custom fit.
